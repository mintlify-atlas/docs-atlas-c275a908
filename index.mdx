## What is VITACORE?

VITACORE is a web-based electronic health record (EHR) platform built with **Flask** and **Supabase**. It gives healthcare teams a centralized system to:

- Register and track patients with complete demographic profiles
- Document clinical consultations with structured evolution notes
- Record vital signs and automatically compute BMI
- Manage medical professionals, specialties, branches, and offices
- Control access with a role-based permission system
